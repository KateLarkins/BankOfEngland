Wallace Cunningham was born and raised in West Tennessee is the 4th of 6 siblings. He attended the University of Tennessee at Martin where he earned his Bachelor of Science in business administration. Wallace has been in the finance industry for 30 plus years. He currently works as a loan originator for the Bank of England. Wallace has been married to his high school sweetheart Shirley Moore Cunningham for 27 years and they have two sons. The family resides in Milan Tennessee. Wallace serves as the Senior Pastor of Greater Mt. Nebo Baptist Church in Hollow Rock Tennessee. Cunningham has served in this Pastoral role for 18 Years and loves caring for the souls of Gods people. Wallace works with the Distinguished Gentlemen organization in Jackson Tennessee with other business professionals to serve and give back to their community. Finally, Wallace's motto is, "If I can help one somebody as I travel this journey of life then my living will not be in vain."
